Lines & Levels Assessment · Integral Theory
What is this? This assessment maps your developmental profile across multiple lines of intelligence: distinct capacities like emotional maturity, moral reasoning, and spiritual awareness that each grow at their own pace. The framework is drawn from Integral Theory (Ken Wilber / AQAL), which identifies altitude levels from egocentric (Red) through worldcentric (Orange/Green) to integral (Teal/Turquoise).

Your results form a psychograph: a radar chart showing where each line sits on the developmental spectrum. Because lines grow independently, most people are advanced in some areas and trailing in others. There is no "good" or "bad" result; the map is useful precisely because it shows where growth energy is most alive.

    Level Descriptions ▶
    1 · Red (Egocentric)
    Impulsive, power-oriented, survival-focused. "My way or the highway."
    2 · Amber (Ethnocentric)
    Conformist, rule-oriented, disciplined. Values order, tradition, and "us vs. them."
    3 · Orange (Worldcentric-Rational)
    Achievement-oriented, scientific, strategic. Values logic, meritocracy, and individual success.
    4 · Green (Worldcentric-Pluralistic)
    Sensitive, egalitarian, relativistic. Values diversity, feelings, and deconstructing hierarchies.
    5 · Teal (Integral-Systemic)
    Flex-flow, systemic, multi-perspectival. Integrates previous stages and manages complexity without judgment.
    6 · Turquoise (Integral-Holistic)
    Global mind, union of feeling and knowing. Sees the world as a single, living organism.

    Higher Levels (Not Tested)

    These transpersonal stages represent states of being rather than conceptual frameworks and are not measurable via multiple choice.

    Indigo (Para-Mind)
    The beginning of true trans-rational awareness. Intuition becomes a reliable mode of cognition.
    Violet (Meta-Mind)
    Subtle-level awareness. Perception of energy, archetypes, and the soul dimension beyond the physical.
    Ultraviolet (Over-Mind)
    Causal-level awareness. The Witness. Resting in the pure, empty screen on which reality is projected.
    Clear Light (Super-Mind)
    Non-dual. The collapse of the Witness and the Witnessed. Form is Emptiness, Emptiness is Form.
