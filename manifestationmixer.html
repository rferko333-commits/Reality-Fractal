      Frequency Guide

      Binaural Beats

      Binaural beats are produced when each ear hears a slightly different frequency. The brain perceives the difference as a third tone, gently entraining brainwave activity toward the target state. Requires headphones for full effect.

      Delta (1-4 Hz)
      The slowest brainwave state. Associated with deep, dreamless sleep, profound physical restoration, and the release of growth hormone. Most effective for overnight sessions or deep healing work.
      Theta (4-8 Hz)
      The hypnagogic threshold. Present during deep meditation, REM dreaming, and the half-awake state between sleep and waking. Strongly linked to creative insight, subconscious access, and the implanting of affirmations below the critical mind.
      Alpha (8-14 Hz)
      The relaxed, present-moment state. Active during light meditation, daydreaming, and calm focus. Reduces stress cortisol and improves receptivity to suggestion. Ideal for affirmation work during alert, eyes-closed sessions.
      Beta (14-30 Hz)
      The normal waking state. Associated with active thinking, problem-solving, and directed concentration. Higher beta can increase alertness and motivation but may also amplify anxiety in some individuals.
      Gamma (30-50 Hz)
      The highest commonly measured brainwave state. Associated with peak cognition, binding of sensory information, and states of expanded awareness. Research links consistent gamma activity to experienced meditators and heightened clarity of perception.
      Schumann Resonances

      The Schumann resonances are natural electromagnetic frequencies produced by the space between the Earth's surface and the ionosphere. Human brainwave activity evolved within this field, and the base frequency of 7.83 Hz overlaps closely with the theta-alpha boundary.

      7.83 Hz (First Mode)
      The Earth's base frequency, often called the planetary heartbeat. Frequently described as deeply grounding. Its overlap with theta-alpha makes it especially useful for calm, centered awareness and full-body relaxation.
      14.3 Hz (Second Harmonic)
      Falls within lower beta range. Associated with gentle mental activation and light focus while maintaining a connection to the grounding quality of the base resonance.
      20.8 Hz (Third Harmonic)
      Mid-beta. Supports sustained attention and ordered thinking. Useful for productivity-oriented sessions that still want a grounded, natural quality to the background frequency.
      27.3 Hz (Fourth Harmonic)
      High beta. Increases alertness and heightened sensory awareness. Some practitioners use this harmonic for sessions focused on clarity, decisiveness, or overcoming mental inertia.
      33.8 Hz (Fifth Harmonic)
      Bridges high beta and gamma. Associated with vivid focus and heightened perception. Often used in advanced sessions aiming for peak performance states or expanded sensory clarity.
      Solfeggio Frequencies

      Ancient sacred frequencies historically embedded in Gregorian chant and rediscovered through numerological analysis of biblical texts. Used here as pure tones (binaural beat set to 0) or as the base carrier frequency.

      174 Hz
      The lowest Solfeggio frequency. Associated with reducing physical and energetic pain, providing a sense of security, and encouraging the nervous system toward deep safety. Often used as a foundation tone for healing sessions.
      285 Hz
      Linked to tissue regeneration and cellular healing. Thought to influence the body's energetic field to rejuvenate and return to an original, optimal form. Commonly used alongside physical recovery practices.
      396 Hz
      Intended to liberate guilt and fear by dissolving the subconscious patterns that hold them in place. Used for releasing grief, trauma residue, and the deep sense of unworthiness that can resist more direct intervention.
      417 Hz
      Associated with facilitating change and clearing negative imprints from the past. Considered useful for breaking cycles, dissolving energetic blockages, and preparing the field for new information to take hold.
      528 Hz
      Perhaps the most widely used Solfeggio frequency. Called the "miracle tone," it is associated with DNA repair, transformation, and deep confidence. The frequency has been subject to more independent research than most others in this set.
      639 Hz
      The frequency of connection, love, and harmonizing relationships. Used to open the heart, improve communication and empathy, and repair relational wounds. Particularly effective during affirmation sessions focused on love and belonging.
      741 Hz
      Associated with awakening intuition, solving complex problems, and expressing authentic truth. Supports clear-eyed decision-making and creative problem-solving.
      852 Hz
      Linked to the activation of intuition and the return to spiritual order. Thought to replace negative thoughts with a clearer awareness of the present moment, and to open communication with higher aspects of the self.
      963 Hz
      The highest Solfeggio frequency. Associated with crown activation, unity consciousness, and the direct experience of pure awareness. Used in sessions focused on spiritual awakening, expanded identity, and connection to the whole.
